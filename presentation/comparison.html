Catcher criterion

The behavioral regression is caught by the proportional-Tax criterion, not the reconcile invariant: correct Tax evidence yields PASS, while a $0.00 Tax yields FAIL — even though Subtotal + Tax still equals the displayed Total. Per-criterion verdicts and the Tax evidence column above show this contrast directly.

Human-verified artifact provenance

The add-control's accessible name was verified manually from the stored pre-action snapshots during the read-only audit. ProofLoop does not infer bug or mutation state from artifacts; the state labels come only from the human-authored presentation manifest. Self-heal: under the renamed control the executor still located and clicked the add-control by intent.
